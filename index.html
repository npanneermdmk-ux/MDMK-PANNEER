About N. Panneer

"He lived for the people, by the people, and to the people of Gudiyatham."

N. Panneer (d. 15 February 2020) was a respected politician from Gudiyatham in Vellore district, Tamil Nadu. He began his political career with the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) as a Nagara Seyalalar (Town Secretary) and later became a devoted follower of Vaiko after the formation of the Marumalarchi Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (MDMK). A grassroots organiser and orator, he served as Vellore District Secretary for more than 15 years and held a state-level council position within MDMK.

Legacy & Remembering

N. Panneer is remembered as a grassroots leader who worked tirelessly to spread Dravidian ideals across Gudiyatham. His long service as Vellore District Secretary, dedication to organising, and efforts to bring influential voices like Nanjil Sampath strengthened the local political fabric. This website is kept as a living legacy to preserve his memory and inspire future generations.
